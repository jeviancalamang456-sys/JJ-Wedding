Our Story

Some love stories begin with grand gestures. Ours began quietly, through a close friend, a change of schools, and God's gentle plan unfolding without us knowing.

When I transferred to the school where my friend studied, Jerome was already there. In those teenage years filled with curiosity, laughter, and kilig, our paths crossed. At first, it was just glances and small moments, but in my heart, I hoped our paths would draw closer. Wanting to share something he loved, I joined the basketball team, hoping he would see that I, too, could match his sporty spirit.

What followed was a season of patience and effort. Jerome chose to love me the old-fashioned way through ligaw that spoke louder than words. For six months, he showed consistency, sincerity, and care. There were sweet surprises along the way, like the time he asked me to get a calculator from his bag, only for me to discover a piece of chocolate instead, simple, unexpected, and full of intention.

Then came the moment that changed everything. On November 5, 2016, I said yes.

From that day on, our love grew, not always perfectly, but always faithfully. Through high school days, college years, and every season that followed, Jerome showed his love in the quietest yet strongest ways. He made sure I was always safely brought from home to school, and later from home to work. Until today, that simple act remains his promise to care, to protect, and to love me every single day.

His love extended beyond me. With deep respect and genuine care, he embraced my family as his own. Over time, we grew not just as a couple, but as one family, sharing laughter, supporting one another, and standing together through every challenge. His presence became a blessing not only in my life, but in the lives of those I love most.

Through all the years of waiting, growing, and learning, God was always at the center, guiding our steps, strengthening our hearts, and teaching us what true love really means.

Now, with grateful hearts and unwavering faith, we are ready to begin our next chapter.
